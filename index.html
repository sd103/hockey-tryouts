Welcome! Here's how it works

  • Pick an age group (M11, M13...) using the buttons at the top.
  • Score players right in the table — tap the dropdowns to give 1–5 for Overall, Skating, Compete and Hockey IQ. 1 = best, 5 = worst. Scores save the moment you pick them.
  • Goalies have their own table below the skaters in each age group — don't forget them! Their skills are scored as G Skill 1, 2, 3.
  • Tap a player's name to open the full scoring page and add written notes.
  • You can change any score at any time — just tap the dropdown again. Made a mistake? Tap My history to see everything you entered and restore an earlier version.
  • Bad rink wifi? No problem. Scores save on your phone and send automatically when connection returns. A banner shows anything still waiting.
  • Only you and the admin see your scores. Other evaluators can't.
